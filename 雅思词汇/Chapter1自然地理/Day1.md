## 圈层
> **atmosphere**|atmo-sphere
> n.大气层，大气圈；氛围
> 【例】The approaching examination  ==created a tense atmosphere==  on the campus.
> 【短语】
> - atmosphere pressure 大气压
> - working atmosphere工作压力
>

> n.水圈

> **lithosphere**|litho-sphere
> n.岩石圈
